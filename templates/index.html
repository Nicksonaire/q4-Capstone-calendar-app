HARD Goals Calendar
Track your Goals, Reach Your Dreams

A calendar app that helps you manage and achieve your Goals
by implementing Mel Robbins's HARD Goals method.

Habit

Any Goal you set should be able to become a habit in your life, something you enjoy, something you want to do. Trigger: Something to remind you of what you need to do. Action: Something done to reach the goal, done same way at the same time each time. Reward: Something to treat yourself for accomplishing the action.

Alive

The Goal should be something that makes you feel energized, motivated, and Alive after you do it; this feeling can be used as a motivator to keep you moving forward. Count down 5... 4... 3... 2... and when you get to 1, just doing it!

Reachable

When a Goal is reachable, you will make the change stick.Make sure the Goal has a tangible ending, something that you can see yourself being capable of achieving, and make sure that you have a way to remind yourself of that ending to push forward.

Daily

Every day you should have a daily plan to reach that goal.
3 W's: What do you need to acomplish today? Who can help me? When do I need to get it done by?
By answering the 3 W's every day will, you will make steady progess towards achieving your Dreams.
